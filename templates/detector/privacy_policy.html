Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 1, 2025

1. Introduction

    We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information collected through our IoT devices and website application. By using Soilution, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

  • Personal Information: This includes data you provide directly, such as your name, email address, phone number, farm location, and account credentials.
  • Sensor and Usage Data: Our IoT devices collect soil data including nutrient levels (N, P, K), pH levels, moisture content, temperature, and environmental readings.

3. How We Use Your Information

Soilution uses the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide soil analytics and nutrient recommendations.
  • To personalize your user experience and farming insights.
  • To send alerts, notifications, and service updates.
  • To improve our algorithms and service quality.
  • To communicate with you regarding support, feedback, and updates.

4. Legal Basis for Data Processing

    We process your personal data based on your consent, our contractual obligations to provide services, compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate interests in operating and improving Soilution.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share data with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent, such as when collaborating with agronomists.
  • With service providers under strict confidentiality agreements (e.g., cloud hosting partners).
  • To comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

6. Data Retention

    We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

7. Data Security

    Soilution implements industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including data encryption, secure APIs, firewalls, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and request a copy of your personal data.
  • Update or correct your information.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent or object to certain processing activities.

9. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify users of significant changes via email or app notifications.
